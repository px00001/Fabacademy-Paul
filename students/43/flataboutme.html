Corey M. Rice : Fab Academy 2017

A high school science teacher in Mayfield, Ohio, USA. He studied education at Bowling Green State University and the American College of Education. After a typical school day, Corey can be commonly be found coaching his Science Olympiad team, whom he edearingly calls his 'nerd herd.' When he is not teaching, he can be found at home hanging out with his dog, Kona. Named after a hawaiian coffee town. Most importantly, his go-to kareoke song is Johnny Cash's Ring of Fire.

His girlfriend helped write this lovely Bio and she's an improv comedian, who also practices medicine.
